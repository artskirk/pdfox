Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Free plan include? +

The Free plan gives you access to all editing features including text editing, AI text recognition, digital signatures, annotations, stamps, and form filling. The only limitations are that exported PDFs will have a small "PDFOX" watermark, and document sharing is not available.

How does the Pro 24-hour access work? +

When you purchase Pro access for ‚Ç¨4.99, you get unlimited use of all features for 24 hours from the moment of activation. During this time, you can edit and export as many PDFs as you want, without watermarks, and with full sharing capabilities. It's perfect for when you have documents to process and want clean, professional results.

Do I need to create an account? +

No! You can start editing PDFs immediately without creating an account. For Pro access, we only need minimal information to process your payment. Your documents remain private and are automatically deleted after processing.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our payment provider.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption to protect your documents. Files are processed securely and automatically deleted after your session. We never store, share, or analyze your documents.

What if I need more than 24 hours or have special requirements? +

Contact us about our Custom plan! We offer flexible solutions for teams, businesses, and users with specific needs. This includes volume pricing, extended access periods, team features, and API access.

Can I get a refund? +

If you experience any technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please contact our support team. We're committed to ensuring you have a positive experience with PDFOX.
