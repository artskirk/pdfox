Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At PDFOX, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our PDF editing service.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and billing information when you create an account
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • PDF Documents: Files you upload to edit (temporarily stored and encrypted)
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, pages visited, time spent, actions taken
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Processing your PDF files, providing editing features, maintaining your account
  • Billing and Payments: Processing transactions and managing subscriptions
  • Communication: Sending service updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage to enhance features and user experience
  • Security: Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and protecting our systems
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and responding to legal requests

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: All files are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on Cloudflare's enterprise-grade network
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access policies and authentication requirements
  • File Deletion: Uploaded files are automatically deleted after processing (or after 30 days for saved files)
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate (payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics) - bound by strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or court order
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale (with advance notice to you)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (service emails still required)
  • Object: Object to processing for certain purposes

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@pdfox.cloud

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Session management, security, authentication (required)
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with our service (optional)
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings and choices (optional)

You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
  • PDF Files: Deleted immediately after processing (free tier) or after 30 days (paid tiers)
  • Billing Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 3 years

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (Standard Contractual Clauses, Privacy Shield frameworks).

9. Children's Privacy

PDFOX is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or prominent notice on our website. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices? Contact us:

  • Email: privacy@pdfox.cloud
  • Address: [Your Business Address]
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@pdfox.cloud
